| StoryTitle1         = Part 3: Master of the Lagoon
| Synopsis1           = On the day of the Sanctuary massacre, [[Lagoon Boy (Prime Earth)|Lagoon Boy]] had been in the Sanctuary's simulation reliving a past event wherein Power Boy was stabbed and he was shot with a laser; he had replayed this simulation more than '''three-hundred thirty-seven''' times, according to the Sanctuary AI. [[Wally West (Prime Earth)|Wally West]] had requested for the simulation to generate his old backyard along with his wife [[Linda Park (New Earth)|Linda Park]], and his children [[Jai West (New Earth)|Jai]] and [[Iris West II (New Earth)|Iris West]], so that he may spend his time with them as he had before [[Flashpoint]].

[[Michael Carter (Prime Earth)|Booster Gold]] had just arrived at Sanctuary that day, and was given a mask and robes for anonymity. In the simulation room, he simply generated a hologram of himself and got into an argument with it. The fight was interrupted by alarms at Sanctuary and Wally was seen running outside; Lagoon boy complied with the alarm instructing him to exit the building and found [[Red Devil]], [[Hot Spot]] and [[Gunfire]]'s lifeless bodies out in the surrounding field. As he stared at them in disbelief, he was struck by a sharpened projectile and killed, weakly laughing as his life ended.

A grief-stricken Flash sat on the porch holding [[Arsenal]]'s body, telling him he just didn't want to be alone. Harley Quinn approached from behind and knocked him unconscious with her mallet, witnessed only by Booster Gold; Harley asked him how his first day had gone, and he dejectedly but simply responded that everyone is dead.

[[Wally West (Prime Earth)|Wally West]] had requested for the simulation to generate his old backyard along with his wife [[Linda Park (New Earth)|Linda Park]], and his children [[Jai West (New Earth)|Jai]] and [[Iris West II (New Earth)|Iris West]], so that he may spend his time with them as he had before [[Flashpoint]].

[[Michael Carter (Prime Earth)|Booster Gold]] had just arrived at Sanctuary that day, and was given a mask and robes for anonymity. In the simulation room, he simply generated a hologram of himself and got into an argument with it. The fight was interrupted by alarms at Sanctuary and Wally was seen running outside; Lagoon boy complied with the alarm instructing him to exit the building and found [[Red Devil]], [[Hot Spot]] and [[Gunfire]]'s lifeless bodies out in the surrounding field. As he stared at them in disbelief, he was struck by a sharpened projectile and killed, weakly laughing as his life ended.

A grief-stricken Flash sat on the porch holding [[Arsenal]]'s body, telling him he just didn't want to be alone. Harley Quinn approached from behind and knocked him unconscious with her mallet, witnessed only by Booster Gold; Harley asked him how his first day had gone, and he dejectedly but simply responded that everyone is dead.
